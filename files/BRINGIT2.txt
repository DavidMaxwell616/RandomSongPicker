u   =  pull a fourth of a step
puu =  pull a step

"Bring it on Home" is one of my favorite Zeppelin songs.  The song is 
extremely easy if you know how to play guitar.  It sounds best on a strat, I 
think, but it sounds almost as good on a Les Paul.  This tabulature is right.  
Don't look at the other ones.  Don't try to play the other ones.  This the 
best there is.  If you think I'm wrong, call me at (502) 867-1160.
