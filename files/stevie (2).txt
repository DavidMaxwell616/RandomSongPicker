THE STEVIE RAY VAUGHAN GUITAR METHOD
TRANSCRIBED BY MAXXDADDY
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
This lesson will touch on various methods used by Stevie in his
solos. The methods covered will be:

1. BLUES SCALE
      Pride & Joy
      May Had a Little Lamb
2. OPEN STRING BOOGIE RIFFS
      Love Struck Baby
3. HENDRIX
      Voodoo Chile(Slight Return)
      Come On Part III
4. SRV EXCLUSIVE
      Lenny
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

OPEN STRING RIFFS

Stevie often uses open string riffs to give his guitar a twangy,
Texas style sound.

Love Struck Baby:
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

SRV's guitar metho consists of three basic styles:

1. Open Note Blues Riffs
	LOVE STRUCK BABY

2. Blues Shuffle Riffs
	PRIDE & JOY

3. Pure SRV
	LENNY

++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
"Love Struck Baby" is basically the same thematic structure as "Johnny Be Good"
with the twangy, Texan open note riffs that gave Stevie his special sound. Try
using the neck and middle pickups.
