	  The other Lemon Song that they have at FTP isn't complete, so
 again it is up to me, the mighty Zil, to make up for it. 
	  On this song, Jimmy Page uses a Les Paul set to treble. The 
 amp is set to distortion, and at the "quiet part," Jimmy uses a
 delay pedal set for 4 times repetition. I love this song, and it's 
 one of my favorites to play. John Paul Jones really kicks ass in 
 this song, too. Because this is such a long song, part of it is over there-->

							
							The Lemon Song
					  from the album Led Zeppelin II
		by John Paul Jones, Jimmy Page, John Bonham, & Robert Plant
