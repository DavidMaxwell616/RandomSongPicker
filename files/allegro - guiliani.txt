A study by Mauro Giuliani (1781-1828)
Tabbed by Mike Stucka (mstucka@luc.edu)

Notes: Fun little piece, to be played very much allegro. Fingers
play the melody, but the thumb plays bass in a very driving style.
Try playing the bass notes "standalone" first to get an idea of
what the piece is trying to accomplish.  Have fun!  -- Mike
