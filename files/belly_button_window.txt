Belly Button Window
Recorded by Jimi Hendrix

Jimi starts to play a blues shuffle on the opening chord, but he then shifts to a jazzier feel.
The left channel chords are pretty solidly patterned, but the right channel is a loose, wah-wah drenched
solo. I have transcribed most of the signature licks, but the ad libbed feel is imperative to maintain
the mood of the song.

First guitar main riffs:
