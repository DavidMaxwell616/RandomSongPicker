BOHEMIAN RHAPSODY BY QUEEN
transcribed by Maxxdaddy

No, this is not merely the Brian May guitar solo transcription, but rather
a tab of a classical guitar version I heard on the radio. 
However, it includes all of May's guitar work, Mercury's vocal melody 
as well as the piano lines, so it will serve the needs of anyone who wants 
to learn the underlying elements of this masterpiece. I also threw in the 
lyrics for good measure. It has one of the best pseudo-classical outros 
I've ever heard.
(except for maybe Roundabout)
