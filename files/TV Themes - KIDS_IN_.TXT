"Having an Average Weekend"
(Kids in the Hall Theme)
by Shadowy Men on a Shadowy Planet

transcribed by Ted James (tedj@oakhill-csic.sps.mot.com

This is pretty much it.  I know that there is a version on Nevada,
but it's very much incomplete.  And part of it is wrong.  This is the
way that I play it.  The song is pretty simple.

This came from the CD version.  However, on the CD, they play verses
1, 2, and 3 three times.  To me, that gets a bit redundant.  My band
usually plays it twice.

Send any additions, corrections, or comments to the above address.
You can also post them, if you want.
