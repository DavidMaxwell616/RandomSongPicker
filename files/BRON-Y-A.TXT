  I have a book of the first 5 led zep albums (with tab) and
it says that the guitar should be tuned to open E.  I can play
this song a little, and so far as i can tell this is the exact
tuning.  
  If you try this with open E, then the only chords are 
simply barring all six strings at the third, fourth, and fifth
frets (well, not at the same time).  The little intro bit is
simply all six strings at the twelfth fret, then a six string
harmonic on the seventh. The notes played are mostly the e on
the fourth string (fourth fret) and the d on the same string
(second fret) with a lot of pull-off's and hammer-on's.
  If anyone tries this and decides that it works better than
the other posted version, let me know and i'll post the whole
thing in tab.

Bron-Y-Aur by Jimmy Page

Acoustic guitar tuned to CACGCE
with heavy echo present in the recording
and Page lets everything ring.

out how to put them together.
