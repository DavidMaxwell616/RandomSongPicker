Subject: TAB:"Over Now" by AIC (correct!) 

A couple days ago, some guy posted a version of this song which most
of us agreed needed some work, but at least it had the basics right.
The relative tuning for this song is definitely DADF#AD(low to high)
despite many postings of an alternate tuning (DADF#DG).  Believe me
I've tried and it is nearly impossible to play it this way.  Any way,
if your playing along with the album or something, because AIC tunes
1/2 step down always, the actual tuning would be C#/G#/C#/F/C#/F#, but
who has time to do that anyway?  The previous posting of this has the
first part half right at least.
