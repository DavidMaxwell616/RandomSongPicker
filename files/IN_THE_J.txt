Permission obtained to copy tablature for free distribution with this 
notice.

Transcription by Allyn Dimock.  There are doubtless errors.  Please use in 
conjunction with recorded song on "Michael Cooney: Singer of Old Songs"
Front Hall 007.

Rag in C.  4/4 with swing.  Accompaniment transcribed, break is similar
except mm 22-27.  Note the G run is very similar to Blind Blake's version
but the rest of the arrangement is fancier.  Note: it's been a while since
I double checked against the record, so the arrangement could have shifted
slightly for my fingers: Just take the version on the record and put it
through your handy MacFolk (tm) folk processor a few times to get this
version.

This minimal tab can't convey all the nuances necessary to play the
song, but the following notation is used to help somewhat:
h hammer
p pull
w() bend to note in parentheses
s slide in tempo
/ grace note slide up 
\ grace note slide down
b brush multiple strings
* brush and immediately right hand damp.

For melody, lyrics, break, please see recording.
