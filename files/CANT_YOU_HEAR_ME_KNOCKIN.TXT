CAN'T YOU HEAR ME KNOKCING
THE ROLLING STONES
Sticky Fingers

This song is divided in two parts, the first one is the rythm (by 
Keith) and the other is the solo (by Mick Taylor).
The first part is in G tuning (DGDGBD) and the other is in standard tuning.
You should play the rythmic with a DoublePickup on a Telecaster (Custom) 
with the neck pickup.
For the solo, it's a heavy LesPaul sound with sustain, played on the neck 
pickup too. For both sections, a valve amp is required...

So here it is...

b(1t): bend of one tone
^v^v^v^v^v^v: vibrato
s: slide
po: pull off
h: hammer on
