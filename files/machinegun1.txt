Machine Gun by Jimi Hendrix
(Part 1)
transcribed by Maxxdaddy

A uni-vibe pedal is essential to attain the proper sound for this piece.
Flangers, phasers and the like sound nothing short of silly by comparison.

Of course, some of the more complex effects accomplished through feedback are only 
approximated through tablature.

Setup: Fender Stratocaster->Vox Wah Wah->Octavia->Arbiter Fuzz Face->Uni-Vibe->Amp
