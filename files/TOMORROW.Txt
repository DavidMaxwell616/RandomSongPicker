    A				     G     D  C   G 
	The water out of the tap is very .... hot to

   A         		      B	    C
 drink	....	Very hot to drink

Following each C chord in this leadin to the chorus, this riff is played:

E-------------------------|	
B-------------------------|	Parentheses indicate hammering
G--------------3-(5)-0-2--|
D--------3-(5)------------|	
A--3-(5)------------------|	
E-------------------------|	

	This riff is closely followed by the A chord in the next series of  
chords whether its the A-B-C of the leadin or the chorus itself (A-D-C-D) 
after the second verse.	The exception being in the first verse where the
song goes into a soft interlude:

E---5-5-10--12-10-----------8-10--12-10----------|
B---5-5-10--------10-12-10--8-10--------10-12-10-|
G------------------------------------------------|
D------------------------------------------------|
A------------------------------------------------|
E------------------------------------------------|

As mentioned above, the chorus is simply A to D to C and back to D.
